Siu Mai!!!!!!!!

Description:

Siu Mai is a type of dumpling that is commonly found in Hong Kong Dim Sum and with street food vendors.

Unlike elsewhere Hong Kong Siu Mai will feature shrimp and will always be wrapped in a yellow sheet of lye water dough.

Ingredients:

  • Gound pork
  • Sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Shiitake
  • Shaoxing wine
  • White pepper
  • Shrimp

Steps:

  1. Form an "O" with your forfinger and thumb.
  2. Place a wonton wrapper over the "O". Push in 1 heaped teaspoon of Filling and push down into the "O" hole.
  3. Use a butter knife to smear more Filling into unitil level with edge of wonton.
  4. Place on work surface and push down to flatten base and user fingers to shape into a round.
  5. The last step is to steam the dumplings!
