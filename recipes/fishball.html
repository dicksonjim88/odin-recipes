Fishball!!!

Description

Fish balls are one of Hong Kong's most popular and representative "street foods", eaten plain or cooked in a curry sauce.

Readily available in traditional markets and supermarkets, fish balls are also a popular ingredient in hot pot.

Ingredients:

  • 600g fishballs (boiled and cooked)
  • 3 tsp garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 400 ml water
  • 3 tsp cornflour (mixed with 2 tbsp water)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pot, saut√© minced garlic and curry powder until fragrant.
  2. Add the water, oyster sauce, salt, sugar and soy sauce. Bring to boil.
  3. Add in the fishballs and lower the heat to simmer for 3 mins. Remove the fishballs and set aside.
  4. Add in the cornflour solution into the pot. Cook for 1 min while stirring continuously, until the sauce thickened.
  5. Pour the sauce over the fishballs. Serve hot.
